This section collects resources to explore the topic responsibly. It prioritizes **rigorous popular science** and repositories with clear usage terms for visual materials.

## Recommended (high level)

- **NASA Image and Video Library**: historical image archive (useful for illustrations and context).
- **Museums and archives**: exhibitions, timelines, and educational material.
- Books and history-of-science references: to place the Cold War and technological innovation in context.

## How to cite and reuse (quick note)

- If you use images, include **credits** (author/archive/license).
- In text, avoid literal copying and cite ideas/data when needed.
